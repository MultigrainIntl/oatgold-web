Purity Protocol oats

Oat Gold™ starts with a foundation no protocol can replicate: wheat has never been grown on our land. Purity Protocol oats add a rigorous, documented standard on top of that — controlled from seed selection through processing, not corrected after the fact.

What it is

A standard that protects purity at every step.

The Purity Protocol is a comprehensive, well-documented set of practices designed to prevent gluten cross-contact across the entire oat supply chain. Rather than relying solely on sorting or testing at the end, it builds purity in from the beginning — dedicated fields, controlled seed, careful crop rotation, segregated equipment, transport, and storage, supported by inspection and testing.

For manufacturers serving gluten-sensitive and celiac consumers, Purity Protocol oats represent the most rigorous sourcing option for a gluten-free oat program.

Pure from field to facility

How it works

Purity built in, step by step.

Seed & field

Controlled seed and dedicated fields with managed rotation reduce cross-contact at the source.

Harvest & transport

Segregated equipment and handling keep oats separated from gluten-containing grains.

Storage & milling

Dedicated storage and processing lines maintain separation through manufacturing.

Inspection & testing

Documentation, inspection, and testing verify the standard is upheld lot to lot.
