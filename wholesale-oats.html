Rolled Oats

Steamed and flaked oats available in a range of flake thicknesses. A workhorse ingredient for breakfast cereals, granola, snack bars, baked goods, and multi-grain blends. Specify flake size and packaging to match your line.

Best for: cereals, bars, bakery, blends

Steel Cut Oats

Oat groats cut into coarse pieces rather than rolled, delivering a hearty, chewy texture. Ideal for premium hot cereals and texture-forward products where a less-processed profile matters.

Best for: hot cereals, premium retail, foodservice

Oat Groats

Whole, hulled oat kernels with minimal processing — the foundation product for further milling, sprouting, and value-added manufacturing. A clean starting point for custom processing.

Best for: milling, sprouting, custom processing

Gluten-Free Oats

Oats produced under controlled handling to support gluten-free claims and sensitive formulations. Available across formats to suit manufacturers serving the gluten-free category.

Best for: gluten-free brands, sensitive formulations

Purity Protocol Oats

Oats grown and handled under the Purity Protocol — the rigorous, end-to-end standard for gluten avoidance from field through processing. Learn how the Purity Protocol works →

Best for: highest-assurance gluten-free programs
